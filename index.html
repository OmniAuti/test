Dead Cat - Here for the pounce

What Is Dead Cat?

Whether you're new to the stock market or a seasoned trader, timing the perfect moment to buy or sell can be a difficult and stressful task. Dead Cat is a Google Chrome Extension that tracks the market's largest fluctuations and supplies you the information for timing a possible bounce or pullback. Dead Cat carries the burden of screening stocks and compiling technical indicators for you; with even more features and alerts coming in future updates. Nothing is guaranteed in today's volatile stock market, but Dead Cat gives you that paw up.



Dead Cat is for educational purposes only. Nothing displayed in Dead Cat is a recommendation to buy or sell any stock or stock options.

Indicators Within Reach

Diving head first into the stock market can be overwhelming. While using Dead Cat, observe how technical indicators look in real time as the day unfolds and search to see how your favorite stock tickers are doing. Study and follow along until you have mastered how each technical indicator works or continue to let Dead Cat do the heavy lifting. Search for your desired stock tickers and get the same technical indicators that can help you pounce on the best opportunity for you.

What Do I Need?

Customizable Alerts

Currently, Dead Cat only shows stocks from Nasdaq and NYSE markets while highlighting bullish or bearish signals on each stock ticker's technical indicators. In future updates: Don't waste your time with all the nonsense and noise of a busy stock ticker feed. See what you want when you want. If Dead Cat's default settings are not what you're looking for, you'll be able to choose which market, stocks, and technical indicators to be alerted on as the day goes on.

Indicator Alert Examples:

  • Bollinger Bands
  • Relative Strength Index (RSI)
  • Stochastic Oscillator (Fast & Slow)
  • Moving Averages (SMA, EMA, WMA, VWMA)
  • Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
